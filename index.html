My Experience

Interested in design since young, I taught myself some of design tools available at that time and used what I've learnt to work on any school/university projects or charity program. I've been working at the current organization for about 10 years now with many hats, one of them as being the graphic designer—the part that always excite and inspire me. Throughout the year, I've worked a lot in publication layout, create illustrations for climate related programs, social media design materials and production printing. I'm currently studying at Arena Multimedie school to improve myself and to meet people who share the same interests.
